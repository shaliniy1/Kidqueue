Plans & Pricing
Do you offer monthly billing?

Right now plans are annual-only while we're in early access. We do offer periodic 7-day trial promotions so you can explore the full product before fully committing. Request a trial offer to get notified when the next trial is available.

Can I try IdeaBrowser without paying the full year?

Yes — we periodically offer 7-day Starter or Pro trials. Sign up to get notified when the next trial promotion is available, or grab our free plan to start exploring daily.

How can I get notified about trial offers?

We send trial offers to our email list periodically. Join our trial waitlist to be the first to know when trials are available.

What's the difference between Starter, Pro, and Empire?

Starter gives you access to the full idea database (800+ ideas), trends, market insights, and founder-fit tools. Pro adds the Hub build system with project workspaces, AI Research Agent, AI Chat Strategist, a Skills Library with 15+ playbooks, Context System, MCP Connectors, and data exports. Empire includes everything in Pro plus weekly coaching and consulting, monthly AMAs with Greg Isenberg, vibe coding office hours, a full workshop and course library, builder community, IRL events, and $50K+ in tool deals.

Do you offer discounts for students, military, or specific regions?

We don't currently offer student, military, or regional pricing discounts, but it's on our radar for the future. We occasionally run 7-day trial promotions as an alternative way to try the platform at a lower price point. Join the trial waitlist to get notified.

Can I buy individual ideas or reports instead of a full subscription?

This is something we're considering for the future, however it's not currently available.

Why was I charged $499 when I expected a monthly charge?

Our plans are billed annually, not monthly. Stripe shows the monthly breakdown (e.g., '$41.58/month billed annually at $499') which can be confusing, but the actual charge is the full annual amount once per year. If this wasn't clear at checkout and you'd like a refund, email support@ideabrowser.com within 30 days.

Product & Features
How much time will this actually save me?

Each idea in our database represents 50+ hours of research condensed into a 10-minute read. Instead of spending weeks validating an idea through Reddit posts, trend analysis, competitor research, and market sizing, you get it all instantly. With the Hub's AI Research Agent, you can get custom research reports delivered in 30 minutes that would typically take days of manual work. The Skills Library and Context System then help you go from research to execution — offers, landing pages, emails, funnels — all pre-loaded with your idea's context.

What's included in the free plan?

The free plan includes the daily Idea of the Day with full business analysis including market research, founder-fit assessment, and trend insights. It's the same depth of analysis as our paid plans, just limited to one idea per day. You also get access to teaser views of the trends database and market insights.

How many ideas are in the database?

We currently have 800+ fully validated business opportunities in our database, with new ideas added regularly. Each idea comes with comprehensive market analysis, revenue potential, implementation guides, and go-to-market strategy.

What makes these ideas different from other idea lists?

Each idea comes with comprehensive market analysis, revenue potential calculations, implementation guides, required resources, competition overview, and risk assessment. We validate with real market signals, not just surface-level inspiration.

What are AI Research Agent and AI Chat Strategist?

AI Research Agent creates a complete market research report on any idea you drop in — delivered in about 30 minutes. AI Chat Strategist gives you Q&A sessions to pressure-test plans, funnels, and copy. Both are available in Pro (3 research reports and 100 chat sessions per month) and Empire (9 research reports and 300 chat sessions per month). They live inside the Hub alongside your project workspaces and context system.

How do I know which ideas match my skills and budget?

Our founder-fit assessment instantly shows which ideas match your skills, capital, and situation. You can filter ideas by required investment, technical skills needed, and time commitment.

Is my idea private when I use the AI Research Agent?

Yes, your ideas and research reports are completely private. Reports generated by the AI Research Agent are only visible to your account and will never be displayed to other users. While our team may access ideas for troubleshooting purposes, we never use customer ideas as output for other users. We also actively avoid posting 'idea of the day' content that's similar to ideas our users have submitted for research.

How do I download my research report as a PDF?

PDF download is not currently available. However, you can export the JSON data if you're a Pro or Empire member and convert it to a PDF or Markdown file using ChatGPT or similar tools. You can also use MCP Connectors to access your Hub data directly in Claude, Cursor, and other AI tools.

Do you have an affiliate or partnership program?

Not currently, but it's something we're considering for the future. No timeline yet.

Trials, Cancellations & Billing
How do I downgrade or switch plans?

Go to Dashboard → Billing → Manage billing, choose the new plan, and confirm. Any issues with the switch, email support@ideabrowser.com.

If I start with Starter, can I upgrade to Pro later? Will it be prorated?

Yes, you can upgrade anytime and you'll only be charged the difference. Go to Dashboard → Billing → Manage billing, and follow the upgrade steps. The system will prorate your remaining Starter balance and only charge the difference to Pro. If you see the full amount instead of the prorated difference, email support@ideabrowser.com and we'll sort it out manually.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked. Go into Dashboard → Billing → Manage billing, click 'Cancel subscription', and confirm.

How much does it cost to upgrade from Starter to Pro?

It costs $1000 to upgrade from Starter ($499/year) to Pro ($1499/year). The system automatically prorates what you've already paid. Go to Dashboard → Billing → Manage billing, select Pro, and you'll see the exact prorated amount before confirming.

What's your refund policy?

We offer full refunds within the first 14 days if you're not finding value.

Account Management

Check spam, promotions, if you still don't see an email please try again. Still nothing after 10 min? While most emails are delivered within 1-3 minutes, sometimes they can take longer due to a variety of reasons. If you're still having issues, email support@ideabrowser.com and we'll hep you out.

International Coverage
Does the research work for my country?

Idea analyses are global by default. Keyword and trend data skew to where search volume is strongest (often US). In Pro, add your target country in the idea brief and our Research Agent narrows sources to that region.

Idea Exclusivity
If thousands read the same idea, is it still valuable?

While we wish everyone executed on the ideas. The reality is, most people don't act on the ideas - they browse for inspiration or validate existing projects. While thousands might read an idea, only a tiny fraction execute, and even fewer follow through long-term. Plus, most opportunities aren't 'winner takes all' - there are often hundreds of ways to niche down, differentiate, or localize the concept based on your skills and network. For more exclusive angles, our Pro plan offers deeper tools to customize, filter by niche, and research your own ideas. Think of each idea as a starting point to help you spot trends, validate concepts, and carve your own path.

Hub & Empire
What is the Hub?

The Hub is Ideabrowser's build system, available on Pro and Empire plans. It gives you project workspaces where you attach ideas, run AI skills, and manage all your research and assets in one place. The Context System remembers your brand, audience, tech stack, and goals across every interaction — so the 10th use is dramatically better than the 1st.

What are Skills in the Hub?

Skills are proven playbooks built by our team for offers, landing pages, emails, funnels, and more. Each skill reads your project context and builds on previous outputs. There are 15+ skills available, with new ones added regularly.

What does the Empire plan include beyond Pro?

Empire includes everything in Pro plus weekly coaching and consulting sessions, monthly AMAs with Greg Isenberg and industry experts, weekly vibe coding office hours, a vibe coding video course, the full workshop and course library, monthly customer acquisition workshops, the builder network and community, IRL events, and $50K+ in tool deals through 530+ partnerships.

What are MCP Connectors?

MCP Connectors let you generate API keys to use your Hub data (research, context, projects) directly in Claude, Cursor, and other AI tools. Your research and context follow you everywhere you work.
