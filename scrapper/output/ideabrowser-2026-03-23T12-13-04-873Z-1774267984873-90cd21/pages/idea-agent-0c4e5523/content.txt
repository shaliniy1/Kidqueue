Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to get my research report?

During our beta launch, research reports are delivered within 24 hours. We're actively working on reducing this time significantly in future updates.

What's included in the research report?

Your report includes comprehensive market analysis, competitor research, community insights from multiple platforms (YouTube, Reddit, Facebook), business strategy frameworks, financial projections, and actionable recommendations - equivalent to 20+ hours of manual research.

How do you gather and verify the research data?

We use a rigorous 40-step research process that crawls multiple data sources, including social platforms, market databases, and industry reports. Our system automatically verifies data points across sources and includes confidence scores for key insights.

How many ideas can I research?

Pro plan members can research 3 business ideas per month, and Empire members can research 9 per month. We plan to increase these limits in future updates while maintaining our high-quality, comprehensive analysis for each idea.
