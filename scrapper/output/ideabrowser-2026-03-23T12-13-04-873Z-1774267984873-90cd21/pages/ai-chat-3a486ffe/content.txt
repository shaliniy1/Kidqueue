Frequently Asked Questions
How does this make your research more accessible?

Our research reports are incredibly comprehensive (50+ hours per idea), but finding specific answers can take time. Chat lets you instantly access any piece of research through natural questions.

Can I talk to any idea or just specific ones?

You can chat with any business idea in our database. Each has its own conversation with all 50+ hours of research ready to discuss - market analysis, competitors, frameworks, everything.

Are the answers based on your actual research?

Yes! Every answer comes directly from our comprehensive research database. Ask 'What could go wrong?' and you'll get insights from our detailed risk analysis and market research.

How do I actually start using this?

Browse our idea database, find something interesting, and click 'AI Chat' on that idea's page. You'll immediately start accessing that idea's complete research through conversation.

Can I still read the full research reports?

Absolutely! Chat is designed to complement our research reports, not replace them. Use chat to quickly find insights, then dive into the full reports when you need comprehensive details.
