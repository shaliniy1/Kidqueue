Frequently Asked Questions
Has anyone built a business and made money with Ideabrowser? Does it work?

Yes! Members have launched businesses and generated revenue using Ideabrowser. Josh Pigford went from idea to $15K acquisition in 7 days. Beau Johnson generated $489 in revenue in his first 24 hours. Dhruval Golakiya shipped an MVP in 8 hours. Bryan W found a validated opportunity in the database and immediately started building. See all success stories for detailed case studies with proof links.

How much time will this actually save me?

Each idea in our database represents 50+ hours of research condensed into a 10-minute read. The Research Agent does 40+ steps of validation in 30 minutes that would take you days of manual work. Idea Builder generates landing page copy, email sequences, and ad creative in minutes instead of hiring copywriters or spending weeks writing it yourself. Chat Strategist answers your questions instantly instead of spending hours Googling or piecing together advice from multiple sources. Empire members get unstuck in minutes through weekly Vibe Coding office hours instead of spending 5 hours debugging alone. Collectively, Ideabrowser can save you months of research, validation, and building time.

How many ideas are in the database?

We currently have 800+ fully validated business opportunities in our database, with new ideas added daily. All tiers get access to the full database.

What's the difference between Starter, Pro, and Empire?

Starter gives you 800+ ideas, trends, market insights, 20 AI-generated ideas/month, and founder-fit tools to find the right idea. Pro adds the ability to research YOUR OWN ideas with 3 Research Agent reports/month, 100 Chat Strategist sessions/month, Idea Builder for launch materials, and data exports. Empire includes everything in Pro (with higher limits: 500 AI ideas/month, 9 research reports/month, 300 chat sessions/month) PLUS weekly coaching with Jordan Mix, monthly AMAs with Greg Isenberg & experts, Vibe Coding course, builder community, IRL events, and $50K+ in tool deals.

What are AI Research Agent and AI Chat Strategist?

AI Research Agent creates comprehensive market research reports in 30 minutes using a 40+ step automated process. Pro gets 3 reports/month, Empire gets 9/month. AI Chat Strategist lets you pressure-test your ideas with AI strategy sessions - Pro gets 100/month, Empire gets 300/month. Both are Pro and Empire exclusives.

Can I use Ideabrowser if I already have an idea?

Absolutely! Use the Research Agent to validate your idea with comprehensive market research (3 reports/month on Pro, 9/month on Empire). Chat Strategist helps pressure-test your assumptions. Idea Builder generates all your launch materials. Many members use Ideabrowser to validate and build their existing ideas faster.

How do I know which ideas match my skills and budget?

Our founder-fit assessment instantly shows which ideas match your skills, capital, and situation. You can filter ideas by required investment, technical skills needed, and time commitment.

Is my idea private when I use the AI Research Agent?

Yes, your ideas and research reports are completely private. Reports generated by the AI Research Agent are only visible to your account and will never be displayed to other users. While our team may access ideas for troubleshooting purposes, we never use customer ideas as output for other users. We also actively avoid posting 'idea of the day' content that's similar to ideas our users have submitted for research.

What's included with Empire coaching and community?

Empire includes weekly coaching & consulting with Jordan Mix and the Ideabrowser team, monthly AMAs with Greg Isenberg and industry experts, weekly Vibe Coding office hours to unstuck you fast, Vibe Coding video course (start-from-zero for non-developers), workshop library & course archive, monthly customer acquisition workshops, builder network to find technical/marketing partners, cofounder matching, IRL events & workshops, and $50K+ in tool deals (members saved $8.3M this year).

Do you offer monthly billing?

Currently we only offer annual billing. This lets us invest heavily in the product and community while keeping prices accessible. Annual pricing also works out to less than a coffee per day.

Can I upgrade from Starter to Pro or Empire later?

Yes! You can upgrade anytime through your billing dashboard. You'll only be charged the prorated difference between your current plan and the new plan.

What's your refund policy?

We offer full refunds within the first 14 days if you're not finding value. After that, you can cancel anytime and your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked.

What's the Vibe Coding course?

The Vibe Coding course is a start-from-zero video course designed for non-developers. Learn how to ship your product with no coding skills required using AI tools and no-code platforms. Empire exclusive.
