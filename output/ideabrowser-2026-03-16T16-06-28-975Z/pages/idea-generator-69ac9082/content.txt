Frequently Asked Questions
How are the ideas personalized to me?

Our AI analyzes your profile - your skills, experience, budget, time commitment, and goals - then generates business ideas that are uniquely matched to your strengths and constraints.

How many ideas can I generate?

Starter members can generate up to 20 ideas per month (4 batches of 5 ideas). Pro members can generate up to 100 ideas per month. Limits reset on the 1st of each month.

Are these ideas already in your database?

No! These are completely new, AI-generated ideas created specifically for you. They're not in our public database - they're unique starting points matched to your profile.

What if I don't like the ideas generated?

You can regenerate ideas anytime (within your monthly limit), or adjust your profile preferences to get different results. The more specific your profile, the better the AI can match ideas to you.

Can I research these generated ideas?

Yes! Pro members can use our AI Research Agent on any generated idea to get comprehensive market analysis and validation.
