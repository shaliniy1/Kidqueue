Crypto inheritance platform for non-technical wallet holders
⏰
Perfect Timing
🌍
Massive Market
⚡
Unfair Advantage
+13 More

Over $140B in crypto is locked forever. Keys lost. Passwords forgotten. Holders who died without telling anyone how to access their wallets. The tools that exist are built for developers and high-net-worth accounts with dedicated security teams. Cold wallets require tech fluency. A regular person with $30K in Bitcoin and a family that doesn't know what a seed phrase is has no good option.

CryptoLegacy lets holders set up a recovery plan and name beneficiaries in minutes. The platform generates a step-by-step guide that walks a non-technical family member through accessing the assets. No seed phrases to decode. No hardware wallet interfaces to navigate. When the holder goes inactive for a set period, a dead man's switch triggers and delivers encrypted instructions to designated contacts. A spouse who has never touched crypto can follow the guide without calling a lawyer or hiring a consultant.

Start with one wallet integration and a recovery guide builder. Test with 100 holders from crypto estate planning forums and Reddit threads where people ask what happens to their coins when they die. The metric: does a non-technical contact successfully complete the recovery walkthrough in a test scenario? Onboarding needs to take under ten minutes or holders won't finish. The hard part is trust. A product that stores access instructions for crypto wallets has to prove its security model before anyone hands over real credentials.

Plans start at $10/month for a single wallet and scale to $30 for portfolio coverage. Crypto tax accountants are the distribution channel because they sit across the table from holders who need this. Estate planning attorneys who keep getting asked about crypto and have nothing to recommend are the second. Wallet providers who want inheritance features without building them are the partnership play. One integration with a mid-size exchange embeds CryptoLegacy into onboarding for new accounts. A crypto holder who has put this off for two years will pay $10/month the moment setup takes less time than thinking about it.
