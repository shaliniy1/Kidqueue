Frequently Asked Questions
How will this help me avoid wasting time?

Instead of discovering you're not a good fit after months of work, you'll see upfront which ideas don't match your skills, time availability, or budget. Focus your energy on opportunities where you actually have a chance.

Does this work for any business idea?

Yes! Every idea in our database gets compared against your specific profile - your technical skills, industry experience, available time, budget, and personal situation.

What if an idea I love has a low fit score?

You'll see exactly why it's a poor fit and what you'd need to change (skills, resources, time) to make it work. Then you can decide if it's worth the investment or if you should focus elsewhere.

How do I start seeing my fit scores?

Browse any idea in our database and you'll see your personalized fit score right on the idea page. No setup required - it uses the profile information you've already provided.

Can this really predict if I'll succeed?

It can't guarantee success, but it identifies major red flags (wrong skills, unrealistic time commitment, insufficient budget) that often lead to failure. Better to know upfront than discover later.
